Our Menu

We prepare and serve a variety of meals here at the snack corner restaurant, including:
-All soft drinks(Natural fruit juice, mineral water and soda)
-Roasts and Fries
-Street Food
-Romantic Meals
-African Cousine

Our main goal is to offer the above and create a family of customers that will always want to have a taste of the foods over and over again.
