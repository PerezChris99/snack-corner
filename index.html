Our Menu

We prepare and serve a variety of meals here at the snack corner restaurant, including:
-All soft drinks(Natural fruit juice, mineral water and soda)
-Roasts and Fries
-Street Food
-Romantic Meals
-African Cousine
-Breakfasts

Our main goal is to offer the above and create a family of customers that will always want to have a taste of the foods over and over again.

OUR MENU DESCRIBED

In this section, our menu is described in clear detail

Roasts and Fries
tasty and addictive

This category includes roasted and fried foods such as fish, chicken, beef muchomo, white rice, pilao, mayonnaise, fruits and vegetables to round out the meal.

Street Food
yummy

Chapatis, eggs (fried and boiled), cassava (fried and boiled), rolexes, kikomando, chips/fries, and vegetables are examples of common street foods in this category.

Romantic Meals
lovely

In this category, there's a combination of various food types and categories like vegetables, fruits, breakfast foods, fried and roasted foods, but with a romantic theme and touch added to them.

African Cousine
traditional

This category includes most of the well-known traditional foods from the region, such as rice, kalo, yams, irish potatoes, sweet potatoes, cassava, vegetables, chicken, beef, groundnuts, fish, and beans.

Breakfasts
day starters

This category includes simple but filling breakfast foods such as sausages, eggs (fried and boiled), Irish potatoes, vegetables, chapatis, avocados, cassava, and rolexes.

Soft Drinks
soothing

Here we have most commonly known types of soothing soft drinks like; sodas, fruit juice, mineral water, and ginger+pineapple skin juice(commonly known as "munanaasi")
